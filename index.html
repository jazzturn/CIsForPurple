C Is For Purple

(Hide) Instructions

Test and sharpen your perfect or relative pitch with just two buttons!

Hit the SPACE BAR to begin playing. If you think you hear a "C"(as in the musical note C), click the PURPLE BUTTON. If you hear any note other than C, click the RED BUTTON.

Guess correctly and watch your points go up. If not, lose them. You won't be penalized for not guessing at all!

If you're feeling overwhelmed by the loss of points (as we all do sometimes...*insert sad face*), press the SPACE BAR to pause the game (SPACE BAR twice to reset your score...but again, it's no biggie).
